Instructions
----------------

Requires Steamodded.

Unzip and put "RPG Classes" folder in your Mods folder, often located under AppData/Roaming/Balatro/Mods

***If you are getting crashes, it could be because of "Cryptid" Mod. You need to update your steamodded mod in Mods folder to this version: https://github.com/Steamodded/smods/archive/refs/tags/old-calc.zip

***Most likely not compatible with mods that overwrite the vanilla deck skins. Ex) FancyDecks
------------------------------------------------------------------------



Classes generally have 2-3 items, 1-2 Bonus Abilities, Increased specialized RNG, and one negative drawback. 

Every Blind requires 35%+ more money to win since this mod ramps up the player's experience.

+Voucher. Vouchers give 2 options to pick from. When you pick a voucher, there's a 33% chance the leveled up version of that voucher will appear every blind (If you have it unlocked).
