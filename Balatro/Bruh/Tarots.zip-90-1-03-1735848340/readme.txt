If you downloaded the .exe file: 

- To install, replace your original Balatro.exe with the newly downloaded Balatro.exe. 

- Your Balatro.exe can be found by right-clicking the game in Steam and selecting "Browse Local Files". 

Done!

If you downloaded the .zip file: 

- Unzip the .zip

- Using 7-Zip, right-click on your Balatro.exe and select "Open Archive" (You may have to select "Show more options" first in the drop-down) 

- In 7-Zip, open "resources", then "textures" and you'll see two folders - 1x and 2x

- Drag the "Tarots.png" from the 1x folder of your downloaded folder into the 1x folder in 7-Zip

- Drag the "Tarots.png" from the 2x folder of your downloaded folder into the 2x folder in 7-Zip

Done!
